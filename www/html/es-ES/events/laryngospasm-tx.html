Tratamiento
  • Notify team to cease stimulation/surgery
  • Give 100% O2, evaluate ventilation
  • Apply CPAP and jaw thrust
  • Confirm or establish adequte IV access
  • Deepen anesthesia with IV and/or inhaled agents. Consider propofol 1-3 mg/kg IV
  • Deepen anesthesia with IV and/or inhaled agents. Consider propofol mg (1-3 mg/kg) IV
  • Give succinylcholine 0.1-2 mg/kg (if no IV: 2-4 mg/kg IM)
  • Give succinylcholine mg (0.1-2 mg/kg) IV (if no IV: mg (2-4 mg/kg) IM)
  • If bradycardia, give atropine 0.02 mg/kg IV (if no IV: 0.04 mg/kg IM)
  • If bradycardia, give atropine mg (0.02 mg/kg) IV (if no IV: mg (0.04 mg/kg) IM)
  • Consider direct laryngoscopy to secure the airway and/or suction
  • Avoid further patient stimulation during stage 2 anesthesia
  • If further airway instrumentation needed, consider airway topicalization with lidocaine
  • Monitor for negative pressure pulmonary edema (pink frothy secretions). If present, consider ETT, PPV, PEEP, ICU
  • Considere DDx
