Manejo de crisis (si es severa)
  • Notificar al cirujano, pedir ayuda y el carro de paro
  • Position patient with left uterine displacement
  • Increase O2 to 100%
  • Empezar compresiones torácicas 30:2 compression:ventilation ADULT RATIO (100-120 chest compressions/min)
  • Give epinephrine 1 mg IV/IO
  • Administrar ADRenalina MICROgramos (10 MICROgramos/kg) IV/IO
  • If cardiac arrest persists for 4 minutes, then proceed with perimortem cesarean delivery or assisted vaginal delivery
  • Perform advanced cardiac life support including defibrillation if indicated
  • Solicitar ecocardiograma transesofágico
  • Considere ECMO
