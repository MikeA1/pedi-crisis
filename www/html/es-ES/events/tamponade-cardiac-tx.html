Anesthetic Considerations
  • Progressive decrease in SV with an increased CVP → systemic hypotension → cardiogenic shock
  • Goals: maintain sympathetic tone and CO via ↑ HR and contractility/fluid bolus prn
    • Induction: Ketamine (1-2 mg/kg IV), muscle relaxant
    • Induction: Ketamine mg (1-2 mg/kg) IV, muscle relaxant
    • Epinephrine 0.05-0.1 MICROgramos/kg IV bolus or infusion (0.01-0.1 MICROgramos/kg/min)
    • epinephrine MICROgramos (0.05-0.1 MICROgramos/kg) IV bolus or infusion (0.01-0.1 MICROgramos/kg/min)
    • Access: Large bore PIV; arterial line ideal but should not delay treatment in hemodynamically unstable patient
    • Avoid: cardiac depression, vasodilation, ↓ HR; ↑ airway pressure (will ↓ venous return) - so may need small tidal volumes or hand ventilation
