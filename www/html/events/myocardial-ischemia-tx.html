Treatment
  • Improve O2 Supply:
    • Give 100% O2
    • Correct anemia
    • Correct hypotension
  • Decrease O2 Demand:
    • Reduce heart rate
    • Correct hypertension
    • Restore sinus rhythm
  • Drug therapy (rarely needed in peds, consult a pediatric cardiac expert):
    • Nitroglycerin 0.5-5 MICROgrams/kg/min infusion
    • Consider heparin 10 Units/kg bolus, then 10 Units/kg/hour infusion
    • Consider heparin Units (10 Units/kg) bolus, then 10 Units/kg/hour infusion
  • Consider DDx
