Verify DX
Verify Hyperkalemia.
May have:
  • K+>6
ECG changes:
  • Tall peaked T waves
  • Prolonged PR
  • Wide QRS
  • Heart block
  • Sine wave
  • V fib or asystole
Causes of Hyperkalemia:
  • Excessive intake: massive or “old” blood products, TPN, cardioplegia, KCl infusion
  • Shift of K+ from tissues to plasma: crush injury, burns, succinylcholine, malignant hyperthermia, acidosis
  • Inadequate excretion: renal failure
  • Pseudohyperkalemia: hemolyzed sample, thrombocytosis, leukocytosis
