Crisis Management (If Severe)
  • Notify surgeon, call for help and code cart
  • Call for Transesophageal Echocardiography
  • Start chest compressions 30:2 compression:ventilation ADULT RATIO (100-120 chest compressions/min)
  • Give epinephrine 10 MICROgrams/kg IV/IO
  • Give epinephrine MICROgrams (10 MICROgrams/kg) IV/IO
  • If cardiac arrest, go to CARDIAC ARREST
  • Consider ECMO
