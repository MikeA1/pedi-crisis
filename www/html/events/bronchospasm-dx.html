Verify DX - Stabilize Patient
  • Verify Bronchospasm: ↓ EtCO 2, upslope stage III EtCO 2 ↑ airway pressures, ↓ SpO 2
  • Auscultate the chest
    • Equal breath sounds?
    • Endobronchial ETT?
    • Wheezing?
  • Check the ETT
    • Kinked?
    • Secretions/blood in the ETT? Need for suctioning?
  • Consider inhaled albuterol
  • Consider deepening anesthetic
  • If needed, give ketamine 1-2 mg/kg IV
  • If needed, give ketamine mg (1-2 mg/kg) IV
  • If severe, consider EPINEPHrine 1-2 MICROgrams/kg IV (MAX 1 mg)
  • If severe, consider EPINEPHrine MICROgrams (1-2 MICROgrams/kg IV, MAX 1 mg) IV
