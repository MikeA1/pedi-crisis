Treatment
  • Give 100% O2, evaluate ventilation, stop N2O and volatiles
  • Stop local anesthetic
    • What was given? Drug, dose, site? Does this = a crisis or not?
    • If crisis, request intralipid
  • Ensure adequate IV access, monitoring of continuous ECG, BP, and SaO2
  • Avoid propofol, vasopressin, calcium channel blockers
  • To suppress seizures: give midazolam 0.05-0.1 mg/kg IV
  • To suppress seizures: give midazolam mg (0.05-0.1 mg/kg) IV
  • To treat hypotension: give Epinephrine 1 MICROgram/kg IV/IO, as needed, may need infusion 0.02-0.2 MICROgrams/kg/min
  • To treat hypotension: give Epinephrine MICROgrams (1 MICROgrams/kg) IV/IO, as needed, may need infusion 0.02-0.2 MICROgrams/kg/min
  • To treat acidosis: Draw serial ABGs, consider a-line
