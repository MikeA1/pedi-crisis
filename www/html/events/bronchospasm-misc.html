Non-intubated Patient
  • Administer supplemental oxygen
  • Auscultate the chest, differentiate from stridor/extrathoracic airway obstruction
  • Consider inhaled albuterol (with spacer)
  • Consider chest radiograph
  • Consider IV steroids
  • If severe, consider intravenous EPINEPHrine 1-2 MICROgrams/kg IV (MAX 1 mg)
  • If severe, consider intravenous EPINEPHrine MICROgrams (1-2 MICROgrams/kg, MAX 1 mg) IV
  • If severe, consider ICU and/or advanced airway management
