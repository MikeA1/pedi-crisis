Treatment
MH Hotline 1-800-644-9737
  • Get MH Cart, dantrolene, and help
  • Inform surgeon and stop procedure, if possible
  • Stop volatile anesthetic, succinylcholine.
  • Attach charcoal filter. Turn O2 flow to 10 L/min
  • Hyperventilate patient to reduce EtCO2
  • Give dantrolene 2.5 mg/kg IV, rapidly, through large bore IV if possible, every 5 min until symptoms resolve. May need up to 10 mg/kg (if no response at this dose, consider DDx). May have one of two formulations at your hospital:
    • Dantrium/Revonto: Assign dedicated person to mix 20 mg vial with 60 mL non-bacteriostatic sterile water
    • Ryanodex: 250 mg mixed with 5 mL non-bacteriostatic sterile water
  • Give dantrolene mg (2.5 mg/kg) IV, rapidly, through large bore IV if possible, every 5 min until symptoms resolve. May need up to mg (10 mg/kg) (if no response at this dose, consider DDx). May have one of two formulations at your hospital:
    • Dantrium/Revonto: Assign dedicated person to mix 20 mg vial with 60 mL non-bacteriostatic sterile water
    • Ryanodex: 250 mg mixed with 5 mL non-bacteriostatic sterile water
