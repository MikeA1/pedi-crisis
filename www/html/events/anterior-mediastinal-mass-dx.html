Verify DX
High Risk Factors
  • Etiology:
    • Pre-existing Dx of anterior mediastinal mass
    • Hodgkin’s and non-Hodgkin’s lymphoma
  • Clinical signs:
    • orthopnea, upper body edema, stridor, wheezing
  • Imaging findings:
    • tracheal, bronchial, carinal, or great vessel compression
    • SVC or RVOT obstruction
    • ventricular dysfunction
    • pericardial effusion
Anesthetic Plan
  • Perform surgery under local anesthesia, if possible
  • Pre-treat with irradiation or corticosteroids
  • Maintain spontaneous ventilation and avoid paralysis
  • Ensure availability of fiberoptic and rigid bronchoscope
  • Cardiopulmonary bypass or ECMO
