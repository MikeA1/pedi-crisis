Verify DX - Stabilize Patient
  • Verify Local Anesthetic Systemic Toxicity (LAST), may have:
    • CNS symptoms (may not occur): tinnitus, metallic taste, seizures
    • CV collapse: rhythm disturbance, altered consciousness
  • Give 100% O2, evaluate ventilation, stop N2O and volatiles
  • Stop local anesthetic
    • What was given? Drug, dose, site? Does this = a crisis or not?
    • If crisis, request intralipid (available in CCD block cart, DCAM block room, CLI carts, pharmacy)
  • Ensure adequate IV access, monitoring of continuous ECG, BP, and SaO2
  • Avoid propofol, vasopressin, calcium channel blockers
