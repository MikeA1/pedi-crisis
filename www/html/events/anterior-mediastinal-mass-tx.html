Treatment
Airway collapse
  • Increase FiO2
  • Add CPAP for spontaneous ventilation; add PEEP for controlled ventilation
  • Reposition to lateral or prone
  • Ventilate via rigid bronchoscope
Cardiovascular collapse
  • Give fluid bolus
  • Reposition to lateral or prone
  • Ask surgeon for sternotomy and elevation of mass
  • Consider ECMO
