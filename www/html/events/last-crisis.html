Crisis Management (if Severe)

  • Check pulse. If cardiac instability occurs:
    • Start chest compressions (lipid must circulate)
    • Give epinephrine 1-10 MICROgrams/kg IV/IO
    • Give epinephrine MICROgrams (1-10 MICROgrams/kg) IV/IO
  • If cardiac arrest, see CARDIAC ARREST
